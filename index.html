Crazy brownies

Best for coding night

m&m and oreo brownies

It's easy, sweet and you can go crazy with the flavors!
If I want to stay awake during coding night I bake crazy brownies! my recommendation: peanut butter and oreos

Ingredients

  • 200 g dark chocolate
  • 100 g butter
  • 3 eggs
  • 100 g suger
  • pinch of salt
  • crazy toppings (oreos, m&ms, pretzel crumbs, nuts, peanut butter..)

Instructions

  1. preheat the oven to 170℃
  2. spray cooking\oil spray a baking dish
  3. melt chocolate and butter in the microwave
  4. whisk all igredians together
  5. pour the mixture to the baking dish
  6. now it's time to add all toppings- go nuts! (literly)
  7. bake 20-25 minutes
